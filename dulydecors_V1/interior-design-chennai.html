What's Included in Our Budget Home Interior Packages

Modular Kitchen Design & Installation

We design modular kitchens using space-optimized layouts, including L-shaped, straight, or parallel configurations, depending on your kitchen's specific dimensions. Climate-Resistant Materials: To withstand Chennai’s high coastal humidity and monsoon conditions, all core carcass materials utilize high-quality, moisture-resistant BWP (Boiling Water Proof) grade plywood to prevent warping, bending, or swelling over time. Hardware & Countertops: Layouts come standard with durable premium granite or quartz countertops, paired with smooth soft-close hinges and telescopic channels for heavy-duty, long-term durability.

False Ceiling Design

Our false ceiling installations utilize high-grade gypsum board construction to create clean, modern profiles suited perfectly for both living and dining spaces.

  • Integrated Lighting Layouts: Each setup includes a custom electrical plan—covering cove lighting, recessed spotlights, or flat panel lighting.
  • Zero-Rework Planning: Electrical points are fully mapped out during the initial 3D design stage, ensuring absolute precision before execution begins and eliminating the risk of costly post-installation rework.

Wardrobe & Custom Storage Solutions

Wardrobes are custom-built on-site for master and guest bedrooms, offering a choice between smooth sliding doors or classic hinged shutter mechanisms.

  • Tailored Loft Storage: Every wardrobe includes custom-fitted top lofts designed to seamlessly match your room's exact ceiling height and proportions, completely avoiding generic, pre-fabricated gaps.
  • Personalized Interiors: Internal configurations—including drawers, dedicated hanging rods, and adjustable shelving—are mapped entirely around your storage needs during our initial design consultation.

Living & Bedroom Styling

Beyond foundational woodwork, we build cohesive room aesthetics by integrating custom TV units, accent feature wall paneling, and strategic space-saving furniture layouts.

  • Floor Space Optimization: Custom furniture placement maps are finalized during the 3D design phase to guarantee that walkways, seating zones, and storage units never crowd your floor plan—a critical step for maintaining an airy feel in compact 2 BHK and 3 BHK apartment layouts.
